FORCEDHALT2

FORCEDHALT2, Receipt of 3 MUPIP STOP signals within xxxx seconds, process: yyyy shutting down with no exit handling like a kill -9

Run Time Fatal: This indicates that a GT.M process recognized the receipt of three MUPIP STOP commands and is shutting down without cleaning up - the equivalent of a kill -9 signal. This event doesn't stop GT.M processes in an orderly fashion, and might produce core dumps.

Action: Determine who initiated the MUPIP STOP and why they did so. Check core dumps(if any) for further analysis.
