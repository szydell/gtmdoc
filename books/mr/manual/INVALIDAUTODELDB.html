INVALIDAUTODELDB

INVALIDAUTODELDB, Autodelete DB must be specified as non-journaled autodb

GDE Error: The AUTODELETE region qualifier cannot be used with a -JOURNAL qualifier.

Action: Use an appropriate combination of qualifiers
