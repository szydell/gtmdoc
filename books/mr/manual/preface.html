The GT.M Message and Recovery Procedures Reference Manual describes the meaning of messages issued by GT.M components, including the M compiler, the run-time system, and utility programs. It serves OpenVMS and UNIX versions of GT.M.

This manual is organized alphabetically according to the message identifier. The meaning of each message is provided as well as suggestions for locating and addressing the cause of error-type messages.
