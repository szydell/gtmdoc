Interprocess Communication

GT.M uses UNIX Interprocess Communication (IPC) resources to coordinate access to the database. Additionally, GT.M includes a daemon process gtmsecshr that implements process wake-up for M locks and clean-up of IPC resources after certain types of abnormal process termination.
