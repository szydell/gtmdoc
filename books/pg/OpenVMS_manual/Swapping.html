SWAPPING

[NO]SWA[PPING]

Enables or disables swapping of the resultant job in and out of physical memory when the job is in a wait state.

For the JOB command to disable swapping of the resultant process, the process issuing the JOB must have the PSWAPM privilege.

By default, JOB enables SWAPPING.
