JNLWAIT

"JNLWAIT"

Causes a process to pause until its journaling buffers have been written. JNLWAIT ensures that GT.M successfully transfers all database updates issued by the process to the journal file before the process continues. Normally, GT.M performs journal buffer writes asynchronously while the process continues execution.

For more information on journaling, refer to the "GT.M Journaling" chapter in the GT.M Administration and Operations Guide.
