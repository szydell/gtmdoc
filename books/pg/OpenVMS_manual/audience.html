Intended Audience

This manual is intended for programmers who develop and/or maintain M applications in the GT.M environment. This manual assumes that the programmers have no previous knowledge of GT.M. However, it does assume that the programmers have access to the OpenVMS documentation that supplements the limited VMS information in this manual.
