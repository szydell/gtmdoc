/[NO]CR[OSS_REFERENCE]

Instructs the Linker to print the global symbol section of the map. The global section shows symbols and the object modules referencing them. This qualifier has meaning only when used with the /MAP qualifier.

By default, the Linker produces /NOCROSS_REFERENCE.
