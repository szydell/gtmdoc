Examples of $QLENGTH()

Example:

    WRITE $DATA(^|"XXX"|ABC(1,2,3,4))
    GTM>0
    SET X=$NAME(^(5,6))
    WRITE $QLENGTH(X)
    GTM>5

Refer to $NAme() section earlier in this chapter for an understanding of the $NAME function.
