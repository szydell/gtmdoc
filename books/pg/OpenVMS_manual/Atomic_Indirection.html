Atomic Indirection

Any expratom or any local or global variable name may be replaced by indirection.

Example:

       GTM>SET x="HOOP",b="x"
       GTM>WRITE a="HULA "__@b
       HULA HOOP
       GTM>

This example uses indirection within a concatenation operation.
