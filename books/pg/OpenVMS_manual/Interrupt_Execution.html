Interrupting Execution

Press <CTRL-C> to interrupt execution, and return to the GTM prompt to continue debugging the program.

Example:

        %GTM-I-CTRLC, CTRLC_C encountered.
        GTM>

This invokes direct mode with a <CTRL-C>.
