$ZREalstor

$ZREALSTOR contains the total memory (in bytes) allocated by the GT.M process, which may or may not actually be in use. It provides one view (see also “$ZALlocstor” and “$ZUSedstor”) of the process memory utilization and can help identify storage related problems. GT.M does not permit $ZREALSTOR to be SET or NEWed.
